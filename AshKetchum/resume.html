My Resume

I have always dreamed of becoming a Pokémon Master and as soon as I hit the age of 10, I rushed to Professor Oak's Laboratory to get my first Pokémon. Originally wanting to choose Squirtle, I ended up getting Pikachu and left on my journey. Starting off as a completely unskilled trainer, I managed to go through, with the assistance of my friends Misty & Brock, and capture new Pokémon and defeat the gyms in Kanto, allowing me to enter in the Pokémon League. Since then, I have continued to travel, make new friends, and capture new Pokémon while still following my dream of becoming a Pokémon Master.
